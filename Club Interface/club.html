DSG - Data Science Group

The Data Science Group (DSG) is a student organization dedicated to fostering innovation through the power of machine learning and data analytics. We aim to provide members with hands-on experience in data science, from data wrangling to machine learning algorithms. Our mission is to equip students with the skills needed to succeed in the rapidly evolving field of data science.
